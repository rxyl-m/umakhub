2. Eligibility

The System is intended exclusively for current iAcademy students, faculty, and staff. Use of a false identity or someone else's credentials is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

3. User Responsibilities

  • You must provide accurate information when registering and reporting items.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.
  • You agree not to submit false, misleading, or fraudulent reports.
  • You must not claim items that do not belong to you.

4. Admin Approval Workflow

All item posts submitted by members are subject to review and approval by an iAcademy administrator before they appear publicly on the board. Admins reserve the right to reject any submission without explanation.

5. Privacy

Contact information provided in item reports will be visible to other users for the purpose of facilitating item returns. The System may log actions including logins, submissions, and approvals for administrative purposes.

6. Prohibited Conduct

  • Posting items that are not genuinely lost or found.
  • Using the System to harass, spam, or defraud other users.
